Suncheol Kim is a minor supporting character in season 1 of Get Schooled. He is a dim witted but a nice man with a heart of gold whom constantly works for the health of his father.

Was taught by his father to not hit women.

In the past, at 17, he was working as a fisherman presumbably under the pretense of helping his father while unknowingly forced to work as a slave of Manchun. He continued for 15 years until the present.

He was arrested for the assault of Manchun as it was too severe. During this, a police officer inside showed him of the village still being terrible and comfort him that he would probably be released. He was soon released as he gain public sympathy of his terrible past. After being released, he returns to the village and says he will live to help the new people who come in and stop the old villagers from continuing Manchun's laws.

Inappears to stopping the residents who are still taking money under the pretext of a village development fund. It seems that he is acting as the village chief.
