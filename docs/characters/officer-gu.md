Appearing in s1, ep.72. Manchun's eldest nephew. He is a village police officer, and his rank is constable. He hates soju.

He is seen in his station when Hyeseung asked for his help in which he asked what happened. However, his uncle came and too Hyseung away.

Later, he was ordered by his uncle to follow Hwajin and Hanlim. He sees them at Hyeseung's house and asked why they were there. After, he went to his uncle place to report while beating Whale. When Hwajin messes up the village, he confronts him but is defeated in one blow. He requested assistance from the military police but they were unable to come due to the typhoon, so the residents attempted to lynch Hwajin and Lim Hanlim, but failed. Afterwards, it is presumed that he was arrested along with the village head.
