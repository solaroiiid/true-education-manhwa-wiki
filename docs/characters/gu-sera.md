Sera Gu is a main supporting character in season 2 of Get Schooled and protagonist of Sera Gu Webtoon. She is the newest warden that was hired by the TRPA

## abilities
- She had never been trained in fighting as her prowess is mainly been a talent.However, she had been trained in Ssireum a bit by her father, thus she also uses grappling when attacking.
- The school she attended in the past was not an ordinary high school, but Gangsan High School, one of the nation's top three hopeless high schools, where she rose to the No. 1 position based on her overwhelming power.
- She had never been really train in fighting as it is just talent. This characteristic is strengthen as her body is very thin. That means it is a very strong type of muscle. Also, judging by the fact that she is said to have a strong constitution due to his family history, it means she has a very strong bone structure. If this is true, then he has a naturally strong fighting ability just from his physical body without needing to learn martial arts, and he is a fighter by nature.
- She defeated Hanlim Lim in an arm wrestling match, and in terms of physical strength alone, she appears to be the strongest among the female characters in Blue String Universe. She had showcased where she displays tremendous strength, going beyond breaking an opponent's ribs or knocking them unconscious with a single blow to launching a person several meters into the air with a single punch or causing a large crack in a thick stone pillar. When seeing her knock down Eunsu Yeo, who boasts incredible physical strength, with a head-on strike, in particular, his resilience is at an incredible level; she was unfazed even when a man much larger than himself, punched him in the face while wearing knuckles, and when her opponent was bare-knuckled, she did not dodge but deliberately took the blows to counterattack. Although she was surprised when stabbed in the abdomen, he subdued and pulled the knife out of her abdomen, and even threatened with it as if it were nothing.
- Unlike her appearance of mindlessly charging in, her intuition is transcendent. Although she was criticized in Get Schooled for attempting to solve every case solely through gut feeling, this instinct does not diminish even in real combat involving fists and weapons; instead, it activates even more swiftly and accurately, allowing her to respond effectively to most difficult enemies. She was able to accurately identify the characteristics and weaknesses of the technique used by Hansol Song—who had mastered the *Gakcheomdo*, just by observing his attack once, bringing him to the brink of countering it.
