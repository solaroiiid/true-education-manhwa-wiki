Hanlim Lim, also called Im Han-rim, is a main supporting character in "Hanlim Gym", a deuteragonist in "Get Schooled", and a minor supporting character in "The Great War". She is one of the key figures in the Blue String Universe.

As the daughter of the owner of Hanlim Gym, she has known martial arts since as a child. One day, she meets Youngha Jeon whom begged her to coach him to be the strongest fighter. Years later, she became a member of the TRPA, a place to correct misbehaving students and corrupted teachers

## appearance
In her youth, Hanlim is a pretty teenager with most her appearance resembles from her mother. She has red eyes and hair that is short. Her outfit is mainly a yellow tracksuit with black linings at the forearms and legs. She has extraordinarily big breasts.

In her current adult years, Hanlim looks slightly different besides the art style, she has longer hair and mainly wears a loose black suit jacket and white business shirt. She also has smaller breasts compared from 8 years ago. It is revealed that her actual hair color is black when she was in the military.

## personality 
Although her personality is rougher than Na Hwa-jin, she is firm yet gentle when you see her treating the victims of Seungyeon High School kindly. Because of that, at first, she was very opposed to Jeon Young-ha's admission to Hallym Gymnasium because she thought he was a scoundrel, but after seeing Jeon Young-ha's rehabilitation, she recognized him and looked after him occasionally, showing that she is not a cold-hearted person. This is also the case at the time of true education, when they first met, she and Yeri Han, who fought each other to the death, commented positively, saying that she did not know that Yeri Han would truly regret it, and went together to save female students from helpers.

In true education, Na Hwa-jin carries a three-stage baton, while Im Han-rim mainly uses a bamboo sword.

The style of true education is also very different from that of Na Hwa-jin. Na Hwa-jin emphasizes the basics of countermeasures and mainly uses words and calculations using violence to reform the offender, and if that does not work, has them face the law as a last resort. However, Im Han-lim's style is to just use force to suppress them.

## combat power
Although she is a woman with physical limitations, she is a powerful figure at the ‘Hallim Gymnasium’ attended by monsters such as Kang Su-ho and Jeon Young -ha. As a former special mission sergeant, she possesses tremendous skills.

She has the agility to easily avoid and counter surprise attacks, the courage to jump off a high-rise building, the power and speed to kill even a strong adult man with a single blow through a vital attack, and the difference in weight class is wide. She also has the sense to overwhelm even in a one-on-one battle against dozens of students on the national team or gangsters holding weapons. She has enough physical strength to pass the same physical strength test as men in the special forces selection test before enlistment . After enlisting, she was said to be one of the top five in terms of mission performance ability and the top in the unit, including Na Hwa-jin , in the field of person-to-person fighting .

As shown above, Na Hwa -jin and Jeon Young -ha are also very highly appreciated by Lim Han -rim's combat power, which is a great part of Nahwajin and Jeon -young, who have a level of combat power in many blushing, and they are highly regarded for the combat power of Lim Han -rim.

In the Daejangjeon , he competed against Yoon Ga-min, and was shown to land several effective hits. Even Yoon Ga-min is surprised by his strength that goes beyond common sense.

Based on this, it can be said that she is by far the strongest female character in Blue String . Bluestring's female characters with combat power include Lee Ji-woo , Hong Ah-seong , Yoo J , and Goo Sera, but with the exception of Goo Sera, they are not at a level comparable to Im Han-rim, either in terms of actual combat experience or impact shown in the story. Goo Sera is described as having a physical advantage over Im Han-rim, but Im Han-rim's true value is his actual combat ability that surpasses even Na Hwa-jin, so there is no possibility that Goo Sera has stronger fighting power than Im Han-rim.

However, since she is a woman, she is not as strong physically as the male characters Na Hwa-jin and Jeon Young-ha , who are the strongest in this area, and appears to faint after being hit by Kang Chi-beom 's brick or being pushed backwards by Hong Seong-hak's fist. She makes up for his relatively weak physical ability with tremendous practical ability.

In addition to this, her training ability as a coach is also at a high level, and the first teacher of Kang Su-ho and Jeon Young-ha was Lim Han-rim.

There is a feeling that she is somewhat undervalued because she is constantly mentioned and described as inferior to Kang Su-ho, but considering Kang Su-ho's strength, this is not a factor that should be greatly undervalued.
