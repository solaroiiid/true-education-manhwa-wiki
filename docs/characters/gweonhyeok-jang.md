Gwonhyeok Jang is a major supporting character and former major antagonist in season 1 of Get Schooled. He was the no.1 of Guun High Tech High School but was on suspension for bringing a weapon.

In the past as a child, he was bullied for his poor family status thus he fights back and felt power from it as they no longer attempt to belittle him. When he joined Guun High Tech HIgh School, he became the first top first-year student in eight years. Has no enemies to the point that even the top tier of other schools are wary of him. He was suspended indefinitely, but thanks to Hwajin's help, the suspension was lifted after two weeks. Prior, he had defeated the Sorok top dog two day ago.
