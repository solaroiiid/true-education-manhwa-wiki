Jincheon Lee
Appearing in s1, ep.51. He is a lieutenant in the Special Forces reserve force and is currently working as the representative of Special Forces Interior, which is made up of special forces soldiers.

He respected Hwajin so much that he hired Dongcheol at his request. Although he was famous as a devil instructor during his time on active duty, he was a very kind person and gave daily wages in an envelope every day. He was also a generous person, giving 200,000 won per day to new recruit Dongcheol. In addition, it seems that a separate bonus will be provided once the building is completed. Later, Dongcheol, who came to his senses and worked hard, was hired as a full-time employee after jail. A few months later, he gives him and the others their pay after completing a finished building. He then congrats to Dongcheol for his complete change to be a respectful father. He greets Hwajin who arrived.

He appears again in s2 ep. 171 as he helps Hwajin deals with Hyungjung. Then he helped stop Mari's TRPA challenge.
