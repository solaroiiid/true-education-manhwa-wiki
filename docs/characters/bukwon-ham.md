Bukwon Ham is a minor antagonist in season 2 of Get Schooled and Sera Gu (Webtoon). He is an officer of Unchained.

Bukwon's appearance is rather ugly. He has a large, fat physique with full arm tattoos. He has rather no neck seeingly due to fat around it. He has an eerie grin with a shaved head.It appears his personality is the same in Get Schooled and Sera Gu.

In Sera Gu (Webtoon), he was the top student at Maengryong High School and a wrestling champion, but he was permanently expelled from the association after causing an accident and reckless behavior. Somepoint became in contact with Hyeontae Seo

In Get Schooled, he is the leader of MZ organization in Seoul.
